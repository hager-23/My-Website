About Me

Having a bachelor's degree in English literature, I have been giving online private lectures since 2020, owing to the fact that I am specialized in analyzing literary works, such as novels and drama, following several literary theories. I believe that literature is of great importance in the sense that when students become emotionally involved, they are motivated, and this, in turn, contributes to their personal development.

Additionally, being an English instructor, I have found modern teaching methods extremely valuable to my field, therefore taking CELTA-based training to enhance my skills as a teacher to assist young learners was remarkably significant in helping me create effective lesson plans.

Apart from that, I have been trained to be an IELTS instructor under the supervision of knowledgeable teachers, two of whom are Laila Akhnoukh, a former IELTS examiner and a current IELTS instructor at AUC and Mohamed Ibrahim, the owner of E-Plus School and an IELTS expert.
